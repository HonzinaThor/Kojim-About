Zasady ochrany osobnich udaju

Posledni aktualizace: 15. dubna 2026

1. Uvod

Tato stranka popisuje, jak aplikace Kojim pracuje s osobnimi udaji uzivatelu.

2. Jake udaje muzeme zpracovavat

  • udaje, ktere zadate primo v aplikaci (napr. profilove udaje),
  • obsah vytvoreny v aplikaci,
  • technicke a diagnosticke udaje nutne pro provoz, stabilitu a bezpecnost.

3. Jak data pouzivame

  • pro poskytovani funkci aplikace,
  • pro opravu chyb a zlepsovani kvality,
  • pro zabezpeceni aplikace a prevenci zneuziti,
  • pro splneni pravnich povinnosti, pokud to vyzaduje zakon.

4. Ukladani a sdileni dat

Data zpracovavame v rozsahu nezbytnem pro provoz aplikace. Pokud vyuzivame overene technicke partnery (napr. hosting nebo notifikace), maji data dostupna pouze v rozsahu nutnem pro poskytovani dane sluzby. Osobni udaje neprodavame.

5. Doba uchovavani

Data uchovavame pouze po dobu nezbytnou k naplneni vyse uvedenych ucelu nebo po dobu stanovenou pravnimi predpisy.

6. Vase prava

Mate pravo pozadovat:

  • pristup k osobnim udajum,
  • opravu nebo vymaz osobnich udaju,
  • omezeni zpracovani nebo vzneseni namitky,
  • prenositelnost udaju tam, kde je to mozne.

7. Kontakt

Dotazy k ochrane osobnich udaju: privacy@kojim.app

Privacy Policy (English)

Last updated: April 15, 2026

1. Introduction

This page explains how the Kojim app handles users' personal data.

2. Data We May Process

  • data you provide directly in the app (for example profile details),
  • content created within the app,
  • technical and diagnostic data needed for operation, stability, and security.

3. How We Use Data

  • to provide app features,
  • to fix issues and improve quality,
  • to keep the app secure and prevent abuse,
  • to comply with legal obligations when required by law.

4. Storage and Sharing

We process data only to the extent required to operate the app. If we use trusted service providers (for example hosting or notifications), they can access data only as necessary to provide their service. We do not sell personal data.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request:

  • access to your personal data,
  • correction or deletion of personal data,
  • restriction of processing or objection to processing,
  • data portability where technically feasible.

7. Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@kojim.app
