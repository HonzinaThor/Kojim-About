Zasady ochrany osobnich udaju

Posledni aktualizace: 15. dubna 2026

1. Uvod

Tato stranka popisuje, jak aplikace Kojim pracuje s daty uzivatele.

2. S jakymi udaji aplikace pracuje

  • udaje, ktere vlozite primo v aplikaci,
  • obsah vytvoreny v aplikaci (napr. zaznamy a nastaveni),
  • lokalni technicka data potrebna pro beh aplikace na vasem zarizeni.

3. Kam se data ukladaji

Data jsou primarne ulozena lokalne ve vasem zarizeni.

4. iCloud synchronizace

Aplikace muze data synchronizovat pouze pres iCloud, pokud tuto funkci pouzivate a mate iCloud na zarizeni aktivni. Synchronizace probiha v ramci vaseho Apple uctu.

5. Analyza, marketing a sdileni

  • aplikace neobsahuje knihovny pro analytiku ani marketing,
  • aplikace neodesila vase vlozena data na vlastni ani externi servery,
  • data nejsou prodavana ani sdilena s tretimi stranami.

6. Smazani dat

Data muzete odstranit primo v aplikaci nebo odinstalovanim aplikace ze zarizeni. Pokud byla aktivni iCloud synchronizace, muze byt potreba data odstranit i z iCloudu.

7. Kontakt

Dotazy k ochrane osobnich udaju: privacy@kojim.app

Privacy Policy (English)

Last updated: April 15, 2026

1. Introduction

This page explains how the Kojim app handles user data.

2. What data the app works with

  • data you enter directly in the app,
  • content created in the app (for example records and settings),
  • local technical data required for app operation on your device.

3. Where data is stored

Data is stored primarily on your device.

4. iCloud synchronization

The app can synchronize data only via iCloud, if you use this feature and iCloud is enabled on your device. Synchronization happens within your Apple account.

5. Analytics, marketing, and sharing

  • the app does not include analytics or marketing SDKs,
  • the app does not send your entered data to our own or third-party servers,
  • data is not sold or shared with third parties.

6. Deleting your data

You can delete data directly in the app or by uninstalling the app from your device. If iCloud synchronization was enabled, data may also need to be removed from iCloud.

7. Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@kojim.app
